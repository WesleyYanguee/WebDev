Chow Mein Noodle

Ingredients

For the sauce:

  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water

For the stir-fry:

  • 8 oz (225g) chow mein noodles (fresh or dried)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 small onion, sliced
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 cup julienned carrots
  • 1/2 cup bean sprouts (optional)
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers (optional)
  • 1 cup cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu (optional)
  • 2 green onions, chopped

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles:
    • If using dried chow mein noodles, cook them according to the package instructions. Drain and rinse with cold water to stop cooking. Set aside.
  2. Make the sauce:
    • In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, cornstarch, and water until smooth. Set aside.
  3. Prepare the stir-fry:
    • Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
    • Add onions and garlic; saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add vegetables and protein:
    • Add cabbage, carrots, and any additional vegetables or protein of your choice. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until tender-crisp. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  5. Cook the noodles:
    • In the same skillet or wok, heat the remaining 1 tablespoon of oil. Add the cooked noodles and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Combine everything:
    • Add the stir-fried vegetables and protein back into the skillet with the noodles. Pour the sauce over the mixture. Toss everything together to coat evenly. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and everything is heated through.
  7. Garnish and serve:
    • Sprinkle with chopped green onions. Serve hot and enjoy!
